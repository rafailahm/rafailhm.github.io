About

Rafael Ahmadov is the youngest Azerbaijani entrepreneur, content creator, and business founder. He is known for starting businesses at a very young age, his bold attitude, and relentless self-confidence. Many entrepreneurs admire his mindset, creative problem-solving, and fearless approach to challenges. Rafael also documents his journey through his video blog, sharing his successes, lessons, and ambitions. Today, he is recognized as one of the most influential young entrepreneurs in Azerbaijan, celebrated for his achievements at such an early age.

Early life

Rafael Ahmadov was born on July 10, 2009, in Baku, Azerbaijan. From an early age, he showed ambition and curiosity that set him apart from his peers. He was born and raised in a poor family, and as he grew up, Rafael faced the usual difficulties of adolescence, but he never allowed age or circumstances to limit his ambitions. From childhood, he dreamed of achieving success, making his family proud, and leaving a mark with his name.

At just 13, Rafael launched his first business ventures, experimenting with online clothing sales and educational courses. He quickly learned the art of selling and attracting clients—skills that fueled his future projects. His bold mindset and fearless approach immediately drew attention from peers and entrepreneurs alike. In school, Rafael excelled academically, teachers praised him for his knowledge but scolded him for his behavior. Even at a young age, the young businessman was distinguished by his boldness and cleverness.

Alongside building businesses, Rafael documented his journey through a video blog, sharing his lessons, achievements, and mindset. By high school, he had established himself as one of Azerbaijan’s youngest and most daring entrepreneurs, known for his creativity, relentless self-confidence, and refusal to follow ordinary paths.

Companies

PracticeX

In early 2025, Rafael Ahmadov founded PracticeX — a platform designed to prepare students for international SAT exam through structured systems, analytics, and AI-driven learning. At the beginning, no one believed in the idea. He built the entire platform himself from scratch, working on it manually for nearly a year — designing, testing, and improving every detail alone. As his vision grew, he decided to form a team to turn it into a global product. To fund PracticeX, he worked as a food delivery courier, sold his personal belongings, and borrowed part of the money. In total, he invested his first $6,000 into the project,at 15 years old. Still, no one believed in it — until the platform generated its first revenue.

Family

Rafael Ahmadov was born into a modest family, yet from his earliest years he carried the mindset of a leader. As the eldest child, he saw himself as a central figure not only within his family, but across his entire lineage. Named in honor of his grandfather, he felt a strong sense of legacy and responsibility from a young age. His father, Elbrus Ahmadov (January 8, 1985), instilled in him hard work, independence, and freedom of choice. Unlike many parents, he never forced a specific path, instead allowing Rafael to choose his own direction while offering guidance and wise advice when needed. His mother, Gunay Ahmadova (March 3, 1987), focused on education and ethics, shaping his discipline and mindset. He also has a sister, Emiliya Ahmadova (December 20, 2012), and a brother, Davud Ahmadov (December 8, 2017). Growing up in a financially limited environment while surrounded by a more affluent society, Rafael understood early that nothing would be given to him. He knew his family would not be able to provide wealth, houses, or cars — and instead of complaining, he accepted this reality and chose to act. At the age of 13, he began his journey. He started selling, reselling, experimenting with business ideas, including clothing and early forms of online trade. Step by step, his projects became bigger, his vision stronger, and his ambition clearer.He was also known for his resourcefulness and coming up with different solutions to solve difficult problems. He built everything independently — from nothing. By the age of 16, he began earning his first significant income. Through discipline, persistence, and self-belief, he transformed his position within the family. Today, Rafael Akhmedov is recognized as a leading figure of his family and surname — a person who elevated his entire lineage through his own efforts. His family and relatives take pride in him as a symbol of leadership, independence, and self-made success.

Performance & Marathon

Rafael Ahmadov is also recognized as a young and influential entrepreneur in Azerbaijan. Beyond business, he actively participates in sports and public events. In 2025, he took part in a major marathon in Azerbaijan with over 20,000 participants, where he stood out as one of the notable young participants. Supporting and encouraging young people to take part in such events, he also gave an interview during the marathon. He regularly engages in football, volleyball, and other sports, maintaining an active lifestyle and spending time each week with close friends and family. At the age of 12–13, Rafael once dreamed of becoming a professional football player. However, realizing his family’s financial situation, he made a conscious decision to shift his focus toward entrepreneurship — choosing responsibility and growth over comfort, and beginning his journey at an early age.

Giving Back

Rafael Ahmadov is actively involved in charitable initiatives across Azerbaijan, providing consistent support to families in difficult financial situations. His efforts focus on helping low-income households, families who have lost loved ones in the war, and those facing serious life challenges. Through regular contributions and personal involvement, he supports families in various regions of the country, delivering essential aid and assistance when it is needed most. For Rafael, charity is not a one-time act, but a continuous responsibility. His mission is simple — to give back, support those in need, and create real impact within his community.
